About me

Sobre mí

Currently, I am studying Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. I enjoy learning and exploring the world of technologies in general, expanding my knowledge and skills. On a personal level, I consider myself a person who enjoys the opportunity to apply my skills to positively impact the people around me.

Graduated in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at UPM, and now working on various projects related to data and artificial intelligence. I'm interested in everything connected to technology, learning new things, and applying what I know in real projects—sometimes for work, sometimes out of curiosity.
